Do I need to install an app? +

No. Vendorfy is fully web-based. Works on any phone browser — iPhone or Android — no download required.

What if my product doesn't have a barcode? +

No problem. Take a photo or enter product info manually. The AI still generates your full listing from there.

How do the QR codes work? +

Each product gets a unique QR to print or display. Customers scan it and see a live product page with your listing, photos, and a way to buy.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account in under 60 seconds.

Starter vs Pro — what's the difference? +

Starter covers up to 100 products. Pro removes all limits, adds AI photo enhancement, and unlocks advanced inventory and lead management tools.
