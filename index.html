Questions

Got
answers

Do I need to install an app?

No. Vendorfy is fully web-based. Works on any phone browser — iPhone or Android — no download required.

What if my product doesn't have a barcode?

No problem. Take a photo or enter product info manually. The AI still generates your listing from there.

How do the QR codes work?

Each product gets a unique QR code you can print or display. When a customer scans it, they see a live product page with your listing, photos, and a way to contact or buy from you.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel directly from your account in under 60 seconds.

What's the difference between Starter and Pro?

Starter is perfect for vendors with up to 100 products. Pro removes all limits, adds AI photo enhancement, and gives you advanced inventory and lead management.

Is this built for mobile use at a booth?

100%. The entire product was designed around the swap meet workflow — scan, list, and manage everything from your phone while you're set up at your table.
